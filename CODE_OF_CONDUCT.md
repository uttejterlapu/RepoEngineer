# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors, maintainers, and community members, pledge to make participation in RepoEngineer a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and collaborative community where everyone feels safe to contribute, learn, and grow.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and welcoming to others.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Demonstrating empathy toward other community members.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Helping new contributors get started.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Collaborating professionally and respectfully.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate intimidation or threats.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.
- Maintaining a respectful and inclusive environment.
- Encouraging constructive collaboration and discussion.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Discussions
- Documentation
- Community forums
- Any other official communication channels related to RepoEngineer

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a private security report or contacting the project maintainers.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled fairly, respectfully, and confidentially whenever possible.

Maintainers will take appropriate action based on the severity of the incident.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

1. Warning
2. Temporary restriction from participating
3. Removal of content
4. Temporary suspension
5. Permanent ban from the community

These actions will be taken with the goal of maintaining a safe and welcoming environment for all contributors.
